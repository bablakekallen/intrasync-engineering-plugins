About DesignLogic

IntraSync Industrial is on a mission to bridge every gap between engineering design and production in the precast concrete industry. DesignLogic is how we bring that mission into the tools engineers use every day.

Our Mission

Eliminating the Gap Between Design and Production

IntraSync Industrial builds software that connects every step of the precast concrete supply chain -- from raw materials and batch plants to engineering design and finished product delivery.

DesignLogic was created because the gap between engineering design and production planning costs the precast industry millions every year in manual data entry, transcription errors, and costly rework. Engineers design in Revit and Tekla, but production teams work from spreadsheets, PDFs, and handwritten notes. That disconnect is unacceptable.

Our plugins live inside the tools engineers already use, capturing design intent at the source and delivering production-ready data downstream -- automatically.

Our Vision

Zero manual data transfer between design and production.

Every parameter, every dimension, every reinforcement detail should flow from the engineer's BIM model directly into production scheduling, batch planning, and quality control -- without a single keystroke of re-entry.

Our Company

Built by People Who Know Precast

IntraSync Industrial is headquartered in Sacramento, California. We were founded with a single purpose: to modernize the precast concrete industry through intelligent, connected software.

Our team is made up of engineers who understand both software development and the realities of precast manufacturing. We have walked plant floors, reviewed shop drawings, and debugged Revit families at 2am. That experience shapes every feature we build.

We are proud members of PCI (Precast/Prestressed Concrete Institute) and NPCA (National Precast Concrete Association), and we actively contribute to advancing technology standards in the precast industry.
