Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does plugin installation take?

Most DesignLogic plugins install in under 10 minutes. Our team provides guided setup to ensure your environment is configured correctly, including Revit add-in manager integration and Tekla extension setup.

Do I need CastLogic ERP to use the plugins?

No. DesignLogic plugins work as standalone tools for any precast engineering team. However, when paired with CastLogic ERP, you unlock seamless design-to-production data flow with zero manual re-entry.

Which Revit versions are supported?

DesignLogic supports Autodesk Revit 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025. We maintain backward compatibility and release updates within 30 days of each new Revit version launch.

Is training included?

Yes. Every DesignLogic license includes onboarding training for your engineering team, plus access to our video library, documentation portal, and dedicated support channel.
