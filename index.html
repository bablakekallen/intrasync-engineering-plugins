Key Capabilities

Everything you need to bridge the gap between design and production in precast manufacturing

BIM-to-Production Pipeline

Extract piece data from 3D models directly into CastLogic ERP. Automated piece marks, dimensions, weights, and material specifications flow seamlessly from design to production.

Automated Shop Drawings

Generate production-ready shop drawings from BIM models with automatic dimensioning, section views, and detail callouts. Reduce drafting time by up to 80%.

Rebar & Strand Detailing

Automatic reinforcement detailing with bar lists, strand patterns, and embed placement. Complete rebar schedules generated from 3D model data.

Piece Tracking Integration

Every piece from design flows into production scheduling, yard management, and delivery tracking. Full lifecycle visibility from model to jobsite.

IFC & openBIM Support

Full IFC 4.0 support for open standards interoperability. Exchange precast data with any IFC-compliant platform without vendor lock-in.

Real-Time Sync

Live bidirectional sync between design tools and ERP. Design changes propagate to production instantly, and production status reflects back in the model.

Precast Connection Library

Pre-built connection details for common precast joints including corbels, dowels, weld plates, and bearing pads. PCI-compliant standard details ready to use.

Weight & Volume Calculator

Automatic weight and volume calculations from 3D geometry. Accurate concrete quantities, lifting weights, and transport loads computed in real time.

Multi-User Collaboration

Multiple engineers work simultaneously on the same project with automatic conflict resolution. Real-time status updates and change notifications across the team.
