Key Integration Points

Three pillars that connect your Revit workflow to every downstream process in your precast operation.

BIM-to-Production

Extract every precast piece from your Revit model with full metadata -- dimensions, volumes, weights, reinforcement, and hardware -- ready for the production floor.

Automated Drawings

Shop drawings generated directly from Revit views with production annotations, rebar details, strand patterns, and dimensioning to your standards.

Live ERP Sync

Real-time bidirectional synchronization with CastLogic ERP. Model changes propagate to scheduling, costing, and inventory without manual re-entry.

PIECE EXTRACTION

Automated Piece Extraction

One click extracts every precast piece from your model with complete production data. No manual takeoffs, no spreadsheet exports, no room for error.

  • Piece marks, dimensions, volumes, and weights extracted automatically from the BIM model
  • Reinforcement schedules per piece including bar sizes, lengths, bend shapes, and quantities
  • Hardware lists and embed locations with coordinates, types, and installation notes
  • Direct export to CastLogic production scheduling -- pieces flow into bed allocation and sequencing instantly

SHOP DRAWINGS

Shop Drawing Automation

Generate production-ready shop drawings directly from your Revit model. Eliminate hours of manual drafting while maintaining full control over every detail.

  • Production-ready drawings generated from Revit views with automatic formatting to your company standards
  • Automatic dimensioning to shop standards with configurable tolerance and notation preferences
  • Rebar placement drawings with bar lists -- complete reinforcement details for every piece
  • Strand patterns and stressing sequences for prestressed members with elongation calculations
  • Hardware and embed detail sheets with installation callouts and bill of materials
  • Revision tracking and management with automatic cloud and delta markers

Erection Planning

Plan the entire erection sequence inside Revit. Visualize crane movements, validate pick weights, and generate the field documentation your crew needs.

System Requirements

Everything you need to get started with the DesignLogic Revit Plugin.

Software

  • Autodesk Revit 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025
  • Windows 10 / 11 (64-bit)
  • .NET Framework 4.8 or later
  • CastLogic ERP connection (optional)

Recommended Hardware

  • Multi-core processor (Intel i7 / AMD Ryzen 7+)
  • 32 GB RAM minimum
  • DirectX 11 capable graphics card
  • SSD with 10 GB free space
