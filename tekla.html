Key Integration Points

Three capabilities that make DesignLogic the standard for precast detailing in Tekla Structures.

Connection Detailing

Complete library of precast connection types with built-in engineering calculations per the PCI Design Handbook. Wet joints, dry joints, and everything in between.

Rebar & Strand

Automatic reinforcement detailing with ACI-standard bend schedules, prestressing strand patterns, and support for GFRP and specialty reinforcement.

IFC Export

Full IFC 4.0 export with precast-specific property sets. Share models with contractors, engineers, and owners with complete data fidelity.

CONNECTIONS

Precast Connection Library

Every connection type your precast operation needs, pre-built with engineering calculations per PCI standards. Select, configure, and place -- the math is already done.

  • Wet and dry connection types including grouted pockets, welded connections, bolted assemblies, and cast-in anchors
  • Bearing pad details with load calculations, material specifications, and placement tolerances
  • Weld plates and hardware connections with capacity checks, weld sizing, and anchor bolt verification
  • Corbel and haunch designs with shear friction, flexure, and bearing capacity calculations
  • Moment-resisting connections for seismic and lateral force resistance per code requirements
  • Engineering calculations per PCI Design Handbook -- automatically generated and documented for each connection

REINFORCEMENT

Reinforcement Detailing

From mild steel to prestressing strand to GFRP, DesignLogic handles every reinforcement type with automatic scheduling, bend details, and production-ready output.

  • Rebar placement with automatic scheduling -- place bars in 3D and get instant bar lists and weight summaries
  • Prestressing strand patterns with debonding, harping, and stressing sequence definitions
  • Post-tensioning duct layouts with profile geometry, tendon forces, and anchorage details
  • Bar bend schedules (ACI standard) with CRSI-compliant bend types and fabrication dimensions
  • Mesh reinforcement support with sheet sizes, laps, and placement drawings
  • GFRP and specialty reinforcement support for corrosion-resistant and non-metallic applications

CAST UNITS

Cast Unit Management

Track every piece from model to production bed. DesignLogic organizes cast units, assigns piece marks, and generates production documentation your plant floor needs.

  • Piece mark assignment and tracking with configurable naming conventions and automatic sequencing
  • Cast unit grouping and sequencing for efficient bed allocation and pour planning
  • Pour-specific reinforcement details showing exactly what goes into each casting operation
  • Embed and hardware tracking per unit with check-off lists for pre-pour verification
  • Bill of materials generation with consolidated rebar, strand, hardware, and concrete quantities
  • Production bed allocation with visual layout planning and optimization suggestions

PRODUCTION

Production Integration

Close the loop between detailing and manufacturing. DesignLogic connects your Tekla model directly to the CastLogic production module for seamless data flow.

  • Direct export to CastLogic production module -- piece data, schedules, and BOMs transfer in one click
  • Automated piece data transfer including dimensions, weights, reinforcement, and hardware for every cast unit
  • Real-time model updates when production changes occur -- RFIs, design changes, and field modifications sync back
  • Quality checkpoint integration -- QC hold points defined in the model carry through to production checklists
  • Shipping and erection data flows from model to dispatch -- load sequencing, trailer assignments, and erection priorities

System Requirements
